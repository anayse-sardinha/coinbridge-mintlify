title: "Apple Compliance Events"
description: "Mandatory email triggers for Provisioning, Inactivity, and Incentives."
---

Apple Pay compliance requires you to send specific communications to users at defined points in the provisioning lifecycle. CoinBridge triggers these as `APPLE_EMAIL_*` events via the [Notify User Event](/docs/webhooks/endpoints/notify-user-event) endpoint.

<Warning>
These email communications are **mandatory** for Apple Pay certification. Failure to implement them will block your app from passing Apple's compliance review.
</Warning>

## Event Types and Required Actions

### Provisioning Success

**`eventType`: `APPLE_EMAIL_PROVISIONING_SUCCESS`**

Triggered when a user's card is successfully activated in Apple Wallet.

**Required action**: Send a confirmation email or SMS within **1 hour** of activation.

---

### Abandoned Flow

**`eventType`: `APPLE_EMAIL_PROVISIONING_ABANDONED`**

Triggered when a user started the "Add to Apple Wallet" flow but did not complete it.

**Required action**: Notify the user within **24 hours** explaining how to complete activation.

---

### Inactive User

**`eventType`: `APPLE_EMAIL_INACTIVE_USER`**

Triggered when a user has provisioned their card but has not made any transaction within 7 days.

**Required action**: Send an email within **7 days** of provisioning highlighting Apple Pay benefits.

---

### User Incentive

**`eventType`: `APPLE_EMAIL_INCENTIVE`**

Triggered when a user has not completed provisioning within 7 days or has not made a first purchase within 14 days.

**Required action**: Offer a minimum **20 ILS** incentive to encourage activation and first use.

## Communications Summary

| Event | Deadline | Channel |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Provisioning Success | Within 1 hour of activation | Email or SMS |
| Abandoned Flow | Within 24 hours | Email or SMS |
| Inactive User | Within 7 days of provisioning | Email |
| User Incentive | At 7 days (no provisioning) or 14 days (no first use) | Email |
