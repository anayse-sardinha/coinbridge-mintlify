---
title: "Solution Overview"
---

CoinBridge is a financial technology solution that leverages patented technology to enable real-world purchases using any digital asset, including loyalty points and rewards. The system transforms these digital assets into usable currency at any shop worldwide that accepts contactless payments. 

By utilizing the standard **NFC/EMV protocol**, customers enjoy a seamless **Tap & Go** experience at the Point of Sale (POS), just like a traditional credit card.
